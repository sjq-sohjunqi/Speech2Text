What is Speech2Text?

Speech2Text is a transcription service that allows users to transcribe audio recordings of their meetings into text format. The system is designed to make the process of logging project meetings a simple task.

Speech2Text also allows users to manage and share these transcripts with other members of their team.

Document your project meetings today with Speech2Text!

Groups

Note: This feature is only available to users who have an account

Users can be organised into groups with different roles.
Each user will be given one of three roles:

  • Owner: Able to delete the group, add/remove users, modify user roles and access group transcripts (if given permission)
    *The creator of the group will be given Owner role
  • Leaders: Add/remove users, modify user roles and access group transcripts (if given permission)
  • Members: Access group transcripts (if given permission)
